Who are we?

Developer club is driven by Tech enthusiasts and open source contributors, we are optimistic and looking forward to conduct workshops and training led by speakers from other technical communities and Student expert.

Why Developer's Club?

“We are different just like you“
Our community believe in providing peer-to-peer learning environment and creating an impact. We are all in for project centric learning, here is your chance to attend workshops, training and even host non-profit events to help others.
